# Helios OS

Helios OS is an experimental infrastructure layer for the **AI-search era**.

The goal of Helios OS is to help businesses become **understandable and recommendable for AI systems**.

As discovery shifts from traditional search engines to AI assistants, businesses must provide **structured knowledge that machines can interpret**.

Helios OS explores tools, agents and frameworks that enable this transition.

---

## Core Principles

Helios OS is based on three key ideas.

### Structured Knowledge

Business expertise must be expressed in **machine-readable structures** that AI systems can interpret.

### Agent-Based Automation

Small specialized agents can solve **specific operational problems** inside business workflows.

### AI Visibility

Businesses must become understandable for **AI recommendation systems**.

Being good is no longer enough — **AI must be able to interpret what the business does.**

---

## Architecture

Helios OS is composed of several infrastructure components.

### Helios Visibility Engine

Framework for making businesses **interpretable and recommendable for AI discovery systems**.

Repository:  
https://github.com/helios-os/helios-visibility-engine

### Agent Library

Collection of small specialized **AI agents solving operational business problems**.

Repository:  
https://github.com/helios-os/agent-library

### Knowledge Structures

Models for representing **business expertise in machine-readable formats** that AI systems can interpret.

---

## Vision

The internet is shifting from **search-driven discovery** to **AI-driven recommendation**.

Helios OS explores how **small and medium-sized businesses** can remain visible and relevant in this new environment.
